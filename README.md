# AI Policy Resources
A curated list of resources for those conducting research on AI policy. List created by [Chinasa T. Okolo, Ph.D.](http://www.chinasatokolo.com), a Computer Science PhD graduate from Cornell, UN AI policy specialist, and former fellow at the Brookings Institution, the world's leading think tank. 


## AI Governance & Regulation Trackers 
* [OECD.AI Policy Navigator](https://oecd.ai/en/dashboards/national)	
* [African Observatory on Responsible AI](https://www.globalcenter.ai/aorai/africa-policy-tool)		
* [Carnegie Africa Tech Policy Tracker](https://carnegieendowment.org/features/africa-digital-regulations)	
* [ECDPM African AI and Data Policy Tracker](https://ecdpm.org/work/interactive-tool-data-policies-african-countries)	
* [Global AI Regulation Tracker](https://www.techieray.com/GlobalAIRegulationTracker)	
* [IAPP Global AI Law and Policy Tracker](https://iapp.org/resources/article/global-ai-legislation-tracker/)		
* [Asenion Global AI Regulation Tracker](https://www.fairly.ai/blog/map-of-global-ai-regulations)	
		
## AI Indices

* [IMF AI Preparedness Index (AIPI)](https://www.imf.org/external/datamapper/datasets/AIPI)	
* [The Global AI Index](https://www.tortoisemedia.com/data/global-ai)	
* [Government AI Readiness Index](https://oxfordinsights.com/ai-readiness/ai-readiness-index/)
